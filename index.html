BACKGROUND INFORMATION

I have a passion for all things creative and a mind like a scientist. I currently reside on South Hill of Spokane, Washington with my son Ellis. I have recently graduated from Eastern Washington University. My major in Visual Communication Design and minor in Studio Art presented itself as the perfect commingling of my logical nature and my intuitive, artistic nature. This gives me the opportunity to create objects with a lasting impact on culture, society, and commercial industries.
